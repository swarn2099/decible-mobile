# Milestones: Decibel Mobile

## v1.0 — Foundation (completed 2026-03-11)

**Goal:** Working mobile app with 3-tab navigation, activity feeds, artist/user profiles, link-paste add flow, GPS check-in, passport redesign, and share cards.

**Phases:** 5 (1-5)
**Requirements:** 49 total, 48 complete
**Last phase:** Phase 5 (Share + Polish)

**Key outcomes:**
- 3-tab navigation with floating pill tab bar
- Link-paste add flow (Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud)
- GPS check-in with stamp animation (Scenarios A+B)
- Passport with Finds grid + Stamps analog section
- Founder/passport share cards + post-found celebration
- Full dark/light mode support

**Deferred:** CHK-03 (Scenario C — unknown venue) → v3.0

## v3.0 — The Living Passport (completed 2026-03-13)

**Goal:** Transform Decibel into a living, social music discovery platform — Glassy Passport redesign, Jukebox social feed, and magic check-in flow.

**Phases:** 4 (6-9)
**Requirements:** 67 total, 66 complete (SHOW-12 deferred)
**Last phase:** Phase 9 (I'm at a Show)

**Key outcomes:**
- Glassy Passport with 3-way collection tabs (Stamps/Finds/Discoveries), frosted glass cards, animated orbs, View More pages
- Jukebox social music feed with embedded players and one-tap Discover collect
- "I'm at a Show" magic check-in with 6-layer scraping waterfall on VM
- Manual fallback form with venue autocomplete + artist link paste

**Deferred:** SHOW-12 (Layer 4 social scraping) — platforms actively block scrapers
